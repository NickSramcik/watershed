Friends of the
Lower Olentangy Watershed

Our mission is to keep the Olentangy River and its tributaries
clean and safe for all to enjoy, through public education,
volunteer activities, and coordination with local decision-makers.

FLOW was formed
as a non-profit
in August, 1997

FLOW By the Numbers (2021)

8,500 Trees planted
49 Litter removal events
45 Infrastructure restoration events
40 Invasive species removal events
11 Tributaries monitored at 17 sites

FLOW Tree Nurseries

FLOW maintains two tree nurseries, one in Worthington on Godown Road next to the dog park, and one in Weinland Park on 8th Avenue. Both are cared for by volunteers.

FLOW Pollinator Gardens

FLOW has installed numerous pollinator gardens in the watershed, in partnership with a number of organizations.
This thriving garden is on Olentangy River Road at the Anheuser-Busch Sports Park. How many species can you identify?
